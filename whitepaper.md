## Introduction

Amaya Token ($AMAYAONE) powers the Amaya Ecosystem — a suite of lifestyle, utility, and community applications designed to promote active living, meaningful participation, and sustainable digital engagement.

The ecosystem initially operates on Web2 infrastructure, with a clear roadmap toward Web3 integration to enhance transparency, decentralization, and on-chain utility.

---

## Ecosystem Integration

### 1. MOVE+
MOVE+ enables users to earn Energy Points through walking or running. These Energy Points can be converted into Amaya Tokens or redeemed for items and services through ecosystem-supported marketplaces.

### 2. Amaya Marketplace
The Amaya Marketplace is a digital platform designed to support creators, partners, and communities by enabling token-based rewards, subscriptions, and engagement-driven interactions.

The marketplace emphasizes transparency, creator empowerment, and sustainable participation within the Amaya Ecosystem.

---

## Amaya Token (AMAYAONE)

Amaya Token ($AMAYAONE) serves as the core utility token for all applications operating under the Amaya Ecosystem.

It is built on the Solana blockchain as an SPL token, ensuring speed, scalability, and low transaction costs.
